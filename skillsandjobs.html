Skills and Jobs Project outcomes/Product Prototype Group Reflection

With sufficient investment, there could be numerous positions that would help increase the appeal and functionalities of the website, bringing the EFly e-commerce website project to another level. However, the team, as EFly’s founders, must round up the most vital aspects needed for the development of EFly, so the most important positions can be identified.
After weighing our options, we will prioritize these 5 main positions, provided that we have sufficient funding:

  1. IT specialists (two people):

    2. The nature of the project obviously means that the competency in the field of Information Technology is undoubtedly valuable. In order to bring the project to the next level, it demands a high level of skill in multiple fields of technology. For this position, we may need to consider more than one person, so the work can be reasonably divided.

    • A web developer will be in charge of making the idea of EFly a reality. A web developer will have the following skills:
      • Website development: Naturally, as EFly itself is an e-commerce website, the specialist’s proficiency in web development and maintenance would be the backbone of our service. Bringing utilities and ideas that benefit the development of the website into reality is the key to success.
      • UX/UI Design: It is very crucial to create a website that is user-friendly and efficient. The professional in charge of this role will have to work in collaboration with the Digital Marketing Specialist to deliver the best outcome. The Digital Marketing Specialist will provide user preferences and help identify current trends, which will serve as a guide for designing the user interface.
      • Data Structure: The website consists of many utilities, so it is important to have a well structured database for control and implementation. In the long run, the project will be developed even further, which is why it requires a structure that is not only efficient but also scalable when the business develops further.
    • A security specialist will be crucial in an e-commerce business. As the website involves databases containing personal information of customers as well as the business’s internal information, developing a secure network is the first thing that must be assured.
      • Layers of security must be set up and maintained regularly to prevent malicious attacks and unauthorized personnels. Implementing a secure website and protecting customer information will prevent the business from possible PR disasters.
      • The website must be constantly maintained so that there is no glitches or errors. For instance, the total cost of an order or a customer’s address must be stored and updated correctly in the database. This will help build up the reputation of the website as a reliable platform, therefore attracting more users.

  2. Business Administrator/Manager:

    3. A business administrator will bring many skills to the table:

    • A good grasp of business & e-commerce law: Understanding business law and legislation is crucial to any start-up to avoid unintentional illegal actions. The administrator will work with the IT department to protect customers rights, and integrity of the business.
    • Human resource: E-commerce consists of many roles with different responsibilities, so it is best to have a HR professional to manage the operation and collaboration of the project’s staff. The administrator will be leading the team in order to achieve our ultimate goal.
    • Communication with potential partners: The administrator will take part in building relationships with business partners.

  3. Data analyst/strategist:

    4. Understanding data from multiple sources (customers, trends, competitors,...) holds an important role in the success of any business. A data analyst will be suitable for this role in our start-up. They will be managing the following tasks:

    • Data mining: The data analyst will work closely with IT specialists to access the latest volume of raw data, which can be processed further to identify valuable insights, patterns or even risks. They will need to have an expertise in utilizing software/services such as SQL, NoSQL, MatLab, Excel,... which are useful tools for manipulating data.
    • Data visualization: this will help other departments see, understand and interact with the data more easily. Transforming information into visually understandable charts/diagrams can help any related department work efficiently with the data.
    • Strategy and decision making: The data analyzed will be further discussed with the Business Manager to help the business make the most optimized, reasonable decision. Leveraging data trends will assist greatly in marketing, as well as influence the process of web development.

  4. Digital Marketing Specialist:

    5. Marketing has always been a very important role in every business that heavily relies on attracting customers. Thus, in the era of social media and online platforms, Digital Marketing has become undeniably vital for any development. Ultimately, marketing is absolutely necessary to bring any business into a big market’s competition. The skills requirements include:

    • Managing search engine optimization (SEO): Using Google’s tool to allow business to be more accessible and having a good position in Google's search bar.
    • Brand management: This skill is extremely vital to create an outstanding online shopping platform in a very competitive market. It may involve designing logos, slogans, etc. that leave a lasting impression on the user.
    • Content creation and social media advertising: By creating engaging content on social platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Tiktok,... to spread the website’s publicity and attract customers.
    • Copywriting: a very efficient method of increasing brand awareness and ultimately persuading potential customers to take actions that benefit the business.
    • Marketing budget management: This position can work with the Business Manager and the Data Analyst to calculate the most cost effective Marketing strategies from the available budget.

    With this ideal team, we will be able to expand greatly on EFly’s scope and reachability. With a stable user base, the team will be able to discuss monetization and long term investments with our investors. EFly will rapidly evolve and reach its true potential with proper implementation and execution of the team.
